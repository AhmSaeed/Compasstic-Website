Our Mission

Our project focuses on casual user. This project introduces an opinion mining and sentiment analysis prototype annotated as “Compasstic”. Compasstic is an opinion mining and sentiment analysis system based on big data concepts. It crawls the social media seeking for polarity and monitor the psychological states in contextual opinions by applying mining and analysis techniques.

The system targets casual users, economists, politicians and psychologists. It aids in their decision making process by providing guidance in their respective domains. The system, deliver either summarized or specialized detailed reports for gathered opinions and emotions based on its user queries.
